Colby Connerly Announces Run for Longview City Council Pos. 2

January 24, 2023

Longview, WA - Today, Colby Conerly made the following statement:

Today I am proud to announce that I am putting my name forward to be one of your next Longview city councilors. I want to make the following clear: I am not compelled to run by the pursuit of power. I am compelled to run to free my neighbors of those who do. Our current City Council undertakes a noble cause of fighting homelessness but in doing so empowers a homeless industrial complex. Meanwhile, they continue to increase the financial burden on working-class families with more taxes while we are in a recession.

For the majority of you who don't yet know me. I grew up in a working-class family, and I have seen what an economic crisis can do to one. My family did not have much but they taught me three essential skills: work ethic, respect, and intuition. Values that council members have lacked these past few years. I have always been one to take bold leaps and never shy from work. College was out of reach for my family so I went to work in retail stores, warehouses, and even a seafood processing plant to pay rent. All the while learning about visual art through independent study. I have since gone on to work as a graphic designer and organizer for several campaigns across the country. It was during this that I realized that local leadership was just as lacking as it is at the national level. Of the 10,000+ doors I knocked on in southwest Washington over the course of 2022 many told me their most pressing issues were at the local level. That's what convinced me to run.

My campaign will be focused on mending the divides caused by poor leadership. To listen to those who are struggling silently among us. The working-class families and small businesses who barely make enough to get by. I set out to create a coalition of reason and respect for all citizens of Longview. Our city already has good foundations. It's time that we build upon them. I look forward to getting to know you all. There will be more to come in the following weeks.
