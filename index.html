About Me

Colby Conerly is a fifth-generation Washingtonian, proud of his roots and deeply committed to serving his community. As a graphic designer based in Longview, Colby brings a fresh perspective to politics, drawing on his diverse experiences and family history in the timber industry. Growing up, Colby’s family weathered the ups and downs of the economy with determination and grit, values that he carries with him to this day. Whether he’s designing campaign materials or crafting a policy proposal, Colby is passionate about creating innovative solutions that resonate with voters and make a real difference in their lives. Having been in Southwest Washington for over a decade, Colby understands the unique challenges and opportunities facing our region and is dedicated to working tirelessly to represent the interests of his community.
